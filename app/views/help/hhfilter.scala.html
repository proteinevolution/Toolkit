Extract a representative set of sequences from an alignment, based of pairwise sequence identity and minimum coverage.
  • Input
    You have the option to enter a multiple sequence alignment (MSA) in FASTA, CLUSTAL, or A3M format. The number of sequences is currently limited to 10,000.

    @help.common.msa() @help.common.a3m()
  • Max. sequence identity (%)
    Filter by maximum pairwise sequence identity, given in percent.
  • Min. seq. identity of MSA hits with query (%)
    Filter by minimum sequence identity with query, given in percent.
  • Minimal coverage with query (%)
    Filter by minimum coverage with query, given in percent.
  • No. of most dissimilar sequences to extract
    Filter most diverse set of sequences, keeping at least this many sequences in each block of >50 columns.
