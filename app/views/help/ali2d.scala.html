Ali2D which takes a sequence alignment as input, performs PSIPRED and MEMSAT2 on each of the sequences and finally plots information about secondary structure, transmembrane regions and amino acids on the alignment.
  • Input
    You have the option to enter a multiple sequence alignment (MSA) in FASTA or CLUSTAL format. The number of sequences is currently limited to 100.

    Multiple sequence alignment

    You can enter alignments in FASTA or CLUSTAL formats.
