PhyML is a software that estimates maximum likelihood phylogenies from alignments of amino acid sequences.
  • Input
    You have the option to enter a multiple sequence alignment (MSA) in FASTA or CLUSTAL format.

    Multiple sequence alignment

    You can enter alignments in FASTA or CLUSTAL formats.
